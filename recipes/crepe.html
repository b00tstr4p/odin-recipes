How to make Crepes

Ingredients

  • 140g plain flour
  • 200ml whole milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 25g unsalted butter, melted, plus a little extra for greasing

Steps

  1. Sift the flour with a pinch of salt into a medium-size bowl and make a well in the middle.
  2. Mix the milk and 100ml of water together. Break the eggs into the well and start whisking slowly.
  3. Add the milk and water in a steady stream, whisking constantly and gradually incorporating the flour as you do so.
